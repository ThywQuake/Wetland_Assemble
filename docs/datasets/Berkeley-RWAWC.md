## Key Points
- Vegetation and clouds can obstruct the view of waterbodies, making accurate, seasonal mapping difficult
- This new CYGNSS-based product combines L-band microwaves with computer vision to produce quasi-global monthly maps of waterbodies
- The product shows greater seasonal and interannual variability than other data sets for new insights into tropical hydrological processes
